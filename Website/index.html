About us

Here you can find out more about our beautiful atelier

ATELIER PERSONA is a premium-class atelier, which has been a leader in individual tailoring and repair of clothing in the CIS since 2000.The success of our atelier is a combination of experience and new technologies, attentive attitude to customers and love for their work. We enjoy our profession. Dozens of our regular customers come to us every day, they chose us for responsible, high-quality and conscientious work. We will be glad to see you among our customers, we are sure that you will appreciate the work of the atelier.The form of clothing may depend on various factors, including the characteristics of the figure, character, age, work activity, the time of year, the environment and other human traits, and other factors. For people who are demanding about their appearance, and there is our clothing repair shop.In our tailoring studio, you can order individual tailoring, tailoring of evening dresses and corporate clothing, tailoring of large-sized clothes and maternity clothes, tailoring of men's, women's and children's clothing, tailoring of outerwear (jackets, coats, raincoats, sheepskin coats, fur coats), sewing curtains and covers for furniture, blankets, and we also accept orders for sewing clothes for animals.

Services

Services that we can do for you

The masters of our atelier are ready to offer you urgent repair of clothing, repair of knitwear, repair, as well as a complete re-cut, with a change in style and the addition of new design solutions for fur coats, sheepskin coats and other outerwear, and leather clothing, costume repair, artistic darning, fitting wedding, graduation and evening dresses, using the most modern methods, materials and equipment. Special discount systems have been introduced for regular customers. Orders are executed on time and in strict accordance with your wishes.
